Research into addiction science includes many different aspects, it is a complex field of study. This site focuses on a common theme in addiction studies, self-esteem.

A Global Problem

Self-esteem, in particular low self-esteem, has been proven to be a main component of addiction. Since most of us experience low-self esteem at times, how we can avoid being pulled into addictive behaviors? Our cognitions of ourselves and our self-talk affect our self-esteem. One strategy that has been proven to help change internal perceptions is Cognitive Behavioural Thearapy a form of psychotherapy you can learn to do it yourself or have a professional help you with it.
